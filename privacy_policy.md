# Privacy Policy for Kalasha Ala Keyboard

**Effective Date:** September 22nd 2025
**Last Updated:** September 22nd 2025

## 1. Introduction

Kalasha Ala Keyboard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile keyboard application ("the App" or "Kalasha Ala Keyboard") handles information when you use our services.

## 2. Age Restrictions

**The Kalasha Ala Keyboard is intended for users aged 13 and above.** Users under the age of 13 should not use this application. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information We DO NOT Collect
- **Keystrokes or Typed Text**: We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to anything you type using the Kalasha Ala Keyboard
- **Personal Messages**: We do not read, store, or transmit your personal messages, passwords, or any text input
- **User Content**: We do not collect any content you create using our keyboard
- **Location Data**: We do not collect your location information
- **Contact Information**: We do not access or collect your contacts, phone numbers, or email addresses

### 3.2 Information We May Collect
- **Device Information**: Basic device model and operating system version for compatibility purposes
- **App Usage Statistics**: Anonymous usage data such as app launches (no keystroke data)
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information if the app crashes, to help us improve stability

## 4. How We Use Information

The limited information we may collect is used solely to:
- Ensure keyboard compatibility with your device
- Fix bugs and improve app performance
- Understand general usage patterns (without any personal data)

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

- **We DO NOT sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties**
- **We DO NOT share typing data with anyone** (because we don't collect it)
- **We DO NOT use your data for advertising purposes**
- We may only disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights

## 6. Data Security

- All keyboard processing happens locally on your device
- No typed content leaves your device through our keyboard
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect any limited data we collect
- We do not store user data on external servers

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Kalasha Ala Keyboard does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services that could access your typing data.

## 8. Data Retention

Since we do not collect typing data or personal information:
- There is no personal data to retain or delete
- Any technical data collected is anonymized and retained only as long as necessary for app improvement
- You may uninstall the app at any time to remove it completely from your device

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Use the keyboard with complete privacy of your typing
- Uninstall the app at any time
- Contact us with privacy concerns
- Request information about what limited data (if any) we have collected

## 10. International Users

The Kalasha Ala Keyboard is designed to work globally. We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) where applicable.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Providing notice through the app store listing
- Any significant changes will be prominently communicated

## 12. Open Source and Transparency

Our keyboard is designed with privacy-by-design principles:
- Minimal data collection
- Local processing only
- No network connectivity for typing data
- Transparent about our practices
